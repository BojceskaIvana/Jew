Jewelry is the decoration of the human body with various made objects, such as: brooches, rings, necklaces, earrings and bracelets. Jewelry can be made from different types of materials but precious stones, precious metals, beads and shells are the ones that are widely used. Depending on the culture and the time in which we live, jewelry can be valued as a status symbol, thanks to its material properties, its patterns or as a symbol with meaning.
